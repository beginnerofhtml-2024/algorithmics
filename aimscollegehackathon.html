About AIMS College

AIMS College is committed to providing high-quality education and developing professionals who will contribute positively to society. With a wide range of courses and dedicated faculty, AIMS is a place where students are inspired to reach their full potential.

Our Courses

BSc CSIT [Bachelors in Science in Computer Science and Information Technology]

BSc CSIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology) is an undergraduate degree program designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer science, information technology, and their applications in various industries. This course prepares students for careers in software development, IT consulting, data analysis, networking, artificial intelligence, and other related fields. The BSc CSIT course combines theoretical concepts with practical skills to equip students with the knowledge required to solve complex technological problems.

BHM [Bachelors in Hotel Management]

BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management) is an undergraduate degree program designed for students interested in pursuing a career in the hospitality industry. The course focuses on providing students with the necessary skills and knowledge to manage hotels, resorts, restaurants, and other hospitality-related establishments. The BHM course combines theoretical learning with practical training to prepare students for leadership roles in the hospitality sector.

BBS [Bachelors of Business Studies]

A BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of business concepts, practices, and management techniques. It is designed to equip students with the necessary skills to pursue careers in business, management, entrepreneurship, finance, and related fields. The BBS course is typically offered by universities or business schools and is popular in countries like Nepal, India, and several others.
